What is Sustainable Energy?

Sustainable energy is energy that is obtained from non-depleting, renewable resources such as the sun, wind, and water. It is environmentally friendly and reduces our dependence on fossil fuels.

Here are some key sustainable energy sources:

  • Solar Energy: Captured from the sun's rays using photovoltaic panels.
  • Wind Energy: Generated by harnessing the power of the wind through turbines.
  • Hydropower: Produced from the flow of water in rivers and dams.

These sources provide clean, renewable energy and play a crucial role in combating climate change.
